Partnership Agreements

If you are working with another individual to put together a service, product or business make sure all the terms and conditions of that agreement are laid out in writing so that all parties involved are protected.

Partnership Dissolutions

Client Contracts

These contracts are needed when you are working with clients. ALWAYS ALWAYS have them in place and signed prior to your relationship. This will help protect both you and your client if something does not go the way that you planned. This is helps you when a client doesn’t pay too.

Consents and Waivers

Have these contracts in place if you are doing any type of body work or work on person. Have them be aware of what you are going to do them and the potential risks involved in that. This will protect you from liability if they are injured accidentally during your sessions.

Affiliate and Referral Fees

Make sure you have an agreement so that everyone understands and is on the same page. This Agreement will provide how the affiliate relationship and referral payments will work, how they can earn and receive payments.

Non-Disclosure Agreements

Protect your private business information. If you have private information or materials in regards to you business that you do not want other people to share. This needs to be signed and in place if you have other member on your team or a potential business partners that you share this private information with. This Agreement will help you know that your information is safe.

Independent Contractor Agreements

Do you have someone working for you and they are not an employee? Get the agreement and expectations in writing make sure the relationship is clear and in writing to set expectations and agreements for the benefit of both of you.

Subcontractor Agreements

Do you have someone working underneath you on a contract that you have? Make sure they meet expectations so that you do not disappoint the person or business that you are contracting with. Do not lose out on future agreements by not having this in place.

Purchase Agreements

If someone is purchasing something from you or you are purchasing from them make sure all of the terms and conditions and warranties are in place. This is the best way to protect your business from future disagreements and preserve future mutually beneficial relationships.

Non-Compete Agreements

This agreement protects if you have someone work for you and they leave your business and so they do not take all of your clients or employees with them if they quit, join another business or start their own. This will save you from losing future revenue or clients.

Service Agreements

If you are provided services or anyone else is providing services to your business make sure that you have all the terms and conditions in writing and that everyone knows the expectations of both parties. This will protect everyone involved and help ensure payments are made if they are in the contract.

Liability Waivers

Dont be held responsible for someone getting injured or hurt in your business, if they are there visiting make sure they sign this agreement so they are held responsible for themselves.

Power of Attorney

This agreement allows you or someone else to make legal decisions on your behalf or on your business’s behalf.

Employee Handbooks

Lays out all of the expectations of your employees and how things will be handled in your business and in the ordinary course of business. This needs to be in place if you have more employees than just yourself working for you or your business.

Social Media Policies

This agreement gives you peace of mind that your employees or contractors will not share certain information on social media in regards to the business or not be allowed on certain sites during the work hours. This will protect your company and reputation in the online world.

Contract Review

Make sure you know what you are signing and the legal ramifications so that you do not sign something that could greatly affect or cause you to lose your business. Become educated so that you can be worry free about what it is that you are signing and agreeing to.
